Harbor

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

The short version

Harbor does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything happens locally on your Mac.

Data collection

Harbor collects no data whatsoever. The app does not contain any analytics frameworks, tracking pixels, crash reporters, or telemetry of any kind. There are no network requests made by Harbor — it operates entirely offline.

Local processing

Harbor reads process information from your system using standard macOS APIs to discover running development servers. This information is displayed to you in the app and is never transmitted anywhere. Theme preferences and session data are stored locally in UserDefaults on your machine and are not accessible to anyone else.

Third-party services

Harbor uses no third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. There is no server-side component to this application.

Children's privacy

Since Harbor collects no data from anyone, it inherently complies with all children's privacy regulations.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date. Given that Harbor collects no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.

Contact

Questions or concerns can be directed to the GitHub issues page.
